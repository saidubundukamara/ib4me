## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 6.1 Confidential Information

The Founder agrees to hold in strict confidence all Confidential Information, including:
- Business strategies, plans, and projections
- Financial information and records
- User, donor, and campaign data
- Technology, code, and platform details
- Partner and vendor agreements and terms
- Marketing strategies and analytics
- Any other proprietary information not publicly known

### 6.2 Obligations

The Founder shall:
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties
- Use Confidential Information only for Company purposes
- Take reasonable measures to protect Confidential Information
- Return all Confidential Information upon departure

### 6.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **3 years**.

### 6.4 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available (not through Founder's breach)
- Was known to the Founder before their engagement
- Is required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice to Company)

## 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 7.1 Company Ownership

All intellectual property created by the Founder in the course of their engagement with the Company shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company, including:
- Software code, algorithms, and technical innovations
- Designs, graphics, logos, and visual content
- Written content, documentation, and marketing materials
- Business methods, processes, and strategies
- Inventions and improvements related to Company business

### 7.2 Assignment

The Founder hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in such intellectual property, including all copyrights, patents, trademarks, and trade secrets.

### 7.3 Moral Rights

To the extent permitted by law, the Founder waives any moral rights in work created for the Company.

### 7.4 Prior Work

The Founder represents that Schedule A lists all prior intellectual property that may be relevant to Company business. Any such IP contributed to the Company shall be licensed or assigned as agreed.

### 7.5 Cooperation

The Founder agrees to cooperate in registering, protecting, and enforcing the Company's intellectual property rights.

## 8. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

### 8.1 Non-Compete

During the term of this Agreement and for **1 year** following termination, the Founder shall not:
- Engage in any business that directly competes with the Company's core crowdfunding platform services in Sierra Leone
- Provide services to competitors of the Company
- Invest in competing businesses (excluding passive investments of less than 5%)

### 8.2 Non-Solicitation of Personnel

For **2 years** following termination, the Founder shall not directly or indirectly:
- Solicit, recruit, or hire any Company employee or contractor
- Encourage any Company personnel to leave their engagement

### 8.3 Non-Solicitation of Business

For **2 years** following termination, the Founder shall not:
- Solicit or divert Company users, donors, or campaign creators
- Interfere with Company partnerships or business relationships
- Use Company contacts for competing purposes

## 9. OUTSIDE ACTIVITIES

### 9.1 Permitted Activities

- Are disclosed to other Founders if substantial

### 9.2 Disclosure

The Founder shall disclose any outside activities that may present a conflict of interest.

## 10. TERMINATION

### 10.1 Termination by Mutual Agreement

This Agreement may be terminated at any time by mutual written agreement of the parties.

### 10.2 Voluntary Departure

The Founder may terminate this Agreement by providing:
- Minimum **60 days** written notice
- Orderly transition of responsibilities
- Return of all Company property

### 10.3 Termination for Cause

The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately for cause, including:
- Material breach of this Agreement or the Founder's Agreement
- Gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Fraud, dishonesty, or criminal conduct
- Persistent non-performance after remediation process (as outlined in Founder's Agreement)
- Breach of fiduciary duties
- Actions harmful to Company reputation

### 10.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- The Founder shall cease all activities on behalf of the Company
- All Company property, documents, and access credentials shall be returned
- Confidentiality and non-compete obligations shall continue as specified
- Equity treatment shall be governed by the Founder's Agreement (vesting, buyback, etc.)

### 10.5 Survival

The following provisions shall survive termination:
- Confidentiality (Section 6)
- Intellectual Property (Section 7)
- Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation (Section 8)
- Dispute Resolution (Section 12)
