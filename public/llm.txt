# ib4me - Medical Emergency Fundraising Platform

## About
ib4me is a crowdfunding platform for medical emergencies in Sierra Leone. The name comes from the Krio phrase "Put fɔ wɛlbɔdi" meaning "contribute to wellness/health."

## Purpose
- Help patients and families raise funds for urgent medical treatments
- Enable donations via mobile money (Orange Money, AfriMoney) and credit cards
- Verify campaigns through medical institutions for trust and transparency

## Key Features
- Medical campaign creation with hospital verification
- Multiple payment methods optimized for Sierra Leone (mobile money) and diaspora (cards)
- Transparent fee structure
- Campaign progress tracking and updates
- Social sharing for WhatsApp, Facebook, and other platforms

## User Actions
- Create a fundraiser: /dashboard (requires account)
- Browse campaigns: /campaigns
- Donate to a campaign: /campaigns/[campaign-slug]/donate
- Learn about us: /about
- View pricing: /pricing
- Contact support: /contact

## Target Audience
- Sierra Leoneans facing medical emergencies
- Family members seeking to help loved ones
- Diaspora community wanting to support from abroad
- Local and international donors

## Location
Sierra Leone, West Africa
