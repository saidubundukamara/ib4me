# IB4ME: Medical Emergency Crowdfunding for Sierra Leone

### Slide 1: Company Overview
**IB4ME** - "In Being For Me" (Put Fo Wɛlbɔdi in Krio)
*Medical Emergency Crowdfunding Platform for Sierra Leone*

**Mission**: To democratize access to life-saving medical care by connecting patients in need with a compassionate community of donors through trusted, transparent crowdfunding.

**Vision**: A Sierra Leone where no one dies from treatable medical conditions due to lack of funds.

---

### Slide 2: The Problem
**Healthcare Crisis in Sierra Leone**

• **Limited Healthcare Access**: Only 35.5% of population has electricity access, severely limiting healthcare infrastructure
• **Out-of-Pocket Healthcare Costs**: Average healthcare spending of only $46 per capita annually vs. global average of $1,000+
• **No Universal Coverage**: Free healthcare only covers pregnant women and children under 5; all others pay full costs
• **Medical Tourism**: Patients travel abroad for complex procedures, costing 10-50x more than local treatment
• **Limited Insurance**: SLeSHI health insurance launched in 2018 but has minimal awareness and coverage

**The Human Cost**:
- 443 maternal deaths per 100,000 live births (down from 717, but still among world's highest)
- Families sell assets, go into debt, or delay treatment due to cost
- Preventable deaths from treatable conditions

**Sierra Leone Healthcare Market**:
- **Population**: 8.5 million (median age 19.7 years)

### Slide 12: Risk Analysis & Mitigation
**Comprehensive Risk Management Strategy**

**Market Risks**:
- **Economic Volatility**: Sierra Leone currency fluctuations, inflation
  - *Mitigation*: Multi-currency support, USD pricing for international donors
- **Limited Internet Penetration**: 30% smartphone adoption
  - *Mitigation*: SMS fallback, community health worker support, offline capabilities

**Operational Risks**:
- **Fraud/Misuse**: Fake medical campaigns, misappropriation of funds
  - *Mitigation*: Hospital verification system, graduated withdrawal requirements
- **Regulatory Changes**: New fintech regulations, healthcare policies
  - *Mitigation*: Close regulatory engagement, legal compliance framework

**Technical Risks**:
- **Payment Platform Dependencies**: Monime outages, mobile money disruptions
  - *Mitigation*: Multiple payment provider integration, redundant systems
- **Cybersecurity**: Data breaches, payment fraud
  - *Mitigation*: Security-first architecture, regular audits, encrypted transactions

**Competition Risks**:
- **International Platform Entry**: GoFundMe, other global players
  - *Mitigation*: Local partnership strategy, cultural advantages, regulatory moats
- **Local Competitor Emergence**: Copycat platforms
  - *Mitigation*: Network effects, hospital exclusivity agreements, brand building

**Financial Risks**:
- **Slow Adoption**: Lower than projected campaign volumes
  - *Mitigation*: Conservative projections, flexible cost structure, pilot program validation
- **Unit Economics**: Higher costs than expected
  - *Mitigation*: Technology automation, operational efficiency, premium service tiers
